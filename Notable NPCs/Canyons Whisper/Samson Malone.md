Samson (Sam) Malone is the proprietor of [Canyon's Whisper's](../../Locations/Canyon's%20Whisper.md) only tavern (currently), The Braying Ass. He and his employee, Woodrow (Woody) Boyd, share bartending duties and chatting with the regulars about town gossip. Sam often talks about his brief athletic career at the capital and the drinking problem that ended it.
