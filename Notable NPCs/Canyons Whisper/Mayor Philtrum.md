While most town mayors live well and eat well, believing their station entitles them to luxury paid for by their citizen's taxes, Mayor Philtrum is not like most mayors. He is one among the people of [Canyon's Whisper](../../Locations/Canyon's%20Whisper.md). He works as hard as they do, lives in the small home, wears his barn clothes a little too much, and exercises his mayoral authority only when absolutely required. 

Philtrum is a muscular, stocky human from his years working the land and managing Canyon's Whisper. He is 6'1" tall, brown hair with streaks of gray in the temples, and yes, he does wear his barn clothes too much.
