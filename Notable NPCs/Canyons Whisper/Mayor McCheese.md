The mayor of [Canyon's Whisper](../../Locations/Canyon's%20Whisper.md). While most town mayors live well and eat well, believing their station entitles them with added luxury, Mayor McCheese is one among the people of Canyon's Whisper. He works as hard as they do and treats his position as a second job.
