Factions discovered during exploration will be recorded here including important information about their allies and enemies.

I will use this section to record not just organized groups but also any sizable group that share something in common. Some examples are a battalion of troops, a thieves guild, a rival adventuring party, and even a group of kobolds housed in a cave.
