Evil acolytes are novices-in-training under a ranking member of a Chaotic sect. While yet unable to employ clerical spells, they are skilled in the arts of battle and are used as bodyguards/cannon-fodder by their superiors. Having heard of [Malfreces Nul](../Notable%20NPCs/Stonehell%20Prison/Malfreces%20Nul.md) powers, their Abbot of Chaos seeks to woo the evil hermit into an alliance with the monastery. It is the acolytes‘ goal to find Malfreces in [Stonehell Prison](../Locations/Stonehell%20Prison.md) and return with him - willingly or not. So far Malfreces has eluded the questing acolytes, leading them into the grasp of the undead in area 1b. He hopes they will eventually give up their quest and leave him in peace.
