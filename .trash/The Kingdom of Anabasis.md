The tyranny of the [Sterling Potentate](The%20History%20of%20Stonehell%20Prison.md) lasted for centuries before the revolutionaries, operating in the shadows, finally stirred the citizens into action. They started in the cities using subterfuge to remove the loyalists from the local government. As each city was freed, the movement gained both momentum and additional members from the local citizens. They moved to the surrounding towns and villages, freeing the people and recruiting more at each settlement. In wasn't long before the rebellion exponentially outnumbered the Potentate's armies.

Led by the secret factions of nobles and common folk that lit the spark, the final battle at the capital was the bloodiest and most costly in the nation's history. Cutoff from resupply, many soldiers, hungry and hopeless, abandoned their posts and attempted escape. Many were caught and killed, by both sides, but a few managed to get away.

The revolution came to an end when the rebels breached the King's estate and slew him in the throne room, spitting vitriol until his final breath.

The dust settled and the Sterling Potentate was removed from power and the kingdom's citizens needed new leadership. In a rare show of unity, the old nobles selected a new leader from the ranks of revolutionaries, One that proved himself intelligent, understanding, and loyal to his country's citizens. The new King was crowned and took the name King Vass Dephrens, first of his name. This first decree was to rename the lands The Kingdom of Anabasis (ah-NAB-ah-sis) for its rebirth and growth.
