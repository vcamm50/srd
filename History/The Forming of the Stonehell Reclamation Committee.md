The new king wants to clean up various areas in the kingdom and bring it under the Kingdom's domain in an effort to make the realm safer as well as undo the damage done by his predecessor. He formed several committees, each comprised of a handful of people willing to oversee this effort on the King's behalf. One group in particular is tasked with the prison known as [Stonehell](Locations/Stonehell%20Prison.md) and have called themselves [The Forming of the Stonehell Reclamation Committee](The%20Forming%20of%20the%20Stonehell%20Reclamation%20Committee.md).
